About Me

Hi! I’m Mohammad Ebrahim Hossain — a passionate storyteller, digital strategist, and change-maker. With a strong academic background in Journalism and Media Communication and hands-on experience in digital marketing, content creation, and feature writing, I thrive at the intersection of communication, creativity, and social impact. I have worked with organizations like 10 Minute School, Newspaper Olympiad, and BabyTube, where I specialized in audience engagement, digital campaigns, and meaningful storytelling. Currently, I am a fellow at the Aspire Leaders Program (Harvard University - Aspire Institute), continuing my journey of personal and professional growth. I’m also leading youth-driven initiatives around climate change and social development, where I combine leadership, innovation, and purpose. I envision a future where words inspire action, brands connect with people, and ideas turn into movements.
